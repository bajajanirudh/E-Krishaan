2. Sir Ganga Ram Hospital

Sir Ganga Ram Hospital is a super-specialty general hospital considered a premier entity in tertiary-care. It is a private charitable hospital managed by Sir Ganga Ram Trust Society offering free treatment to economically weak patients of Delhi and its neighboring states. It is the first hospital to have installed the whole-body CT scan unit and a vascular surgery unit in Delhi.
